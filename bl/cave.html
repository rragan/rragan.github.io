Sayeth the Dragon:

"If to my cave you stray
A dragon's game you must play
I know of a Queen that you seek
So be careful of what you speak
Here is my riddle for you
So tell me the answer true

The Beginning of eternity,
The end of time and space,
The beginning of every end,
And the end of every place.

What am I?"
